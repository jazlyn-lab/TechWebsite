Strong Future Technology Training Center

Empowering the youth and young adults with in-demand tech skills

Welcome to the Strong Future Technology Training Center at the Historic Westside School Campus! Our mission is to engage youth and adults in technology, preparing them for in-demand careers through hands-on, work-based training. Participants can earn certificates and stackable credentials with job placement assistance available. Our state-of-the-art facility features 3D printers, robotics kits, gaming computers, VR headsets, a podcast room, programming software, and high-quality cameras. By providing access to these resources, we aim to inspire creativity, foster learning, and empower our community. Join us at the Historic Westside School and let's build a stronger future together.
